# FinSight – SMB Financial Intelligence SaaS

## Problem Statement
Small and mid-sized businesses struggle to understand their financial health in real time.
Data is scattered across accounting tools, bank statements, and spreadsheets, making
forecasting and decision-making reactive instead of proactive.

## Target Users (ICP)
- SMB founders (10–500 employees)
- Finance managers
- Fractional CFOs

## Core Value Proposition
FinSight provides real-time financial visibility, cashflow forecasting,
and actionable insights to help SMBs make confident business decisions.

## Revenue Model
- Subscription SaaS
- Tiered pricing based on features and business size
- Premium add-ons: forecasting, scenario analysis, investor reporting

## Market Sizing
- TAM: Digitally active SMBs globally
- SAM: SMBs with revenue $1M–$50M
- SOM: Initial focus on AU + APAC SMBs

## Key Business Goals
- Improve retention through financial clarity
- Increase premium feature adoption
- Reduce churn caused by poor onboarding
